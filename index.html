Core Infrastructure

Powered by the NMI Gateway

We do not believe in rebuilding the wheel. POSitiveRails acts as the proprietary "brain" interpreting merchant data, while relying on the absolute stability, uptime, and Level 1 PCI-DSS security of the Network Merchants Inc. (NMI) framework to execute the transaction.

Omnichannel Capability

By utilizing NMI's robust device APIs and ATRI routing, we offer our merchants a unified environment—syncing physical smart terminals, mobile swipers, and global eCommerce checkouts into a single ledger.

Multi-Acquirer Agility

We leverage NMI's gateway to push distinct payload types to specialized acquirers. High-ticket logistics goes to Priority, retail surcharging goes to PayRoc, and digital assets route via alternative APMs.

Zero Data Liability

POSitiveRails operates exclusively on the Data Layer. Raw card data is tokenized immediately via NMI's P2PE hardware integration, completely removing NDPS and our merchants from the PCI scope.

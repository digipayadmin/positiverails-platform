POSitiveRails eliminates aggregator liability. By operating strictly on the Data Layer, we ensure that raw card data and cross-border digital assets are structurally isolated, keeping our acquiring partners perfectly insulated from compliance risks.

PCI-DSS Level 1 Infrastructure

We mandate Point-to-Point Encryption (P2PE) across all OmniPOS edge devices. Raw Primary Account Numbers (PANs) are encrypted at the moment of swipe/dip and tokenized immediately by the NMI gateway. POSitiveRails routes tokens, never raw data.

Scope Reduction: 100%

Digital Asset (Web3) Insulation

Traditional fiat acquirers (Priority, PayRoc) strictly prohibit unverified cryptocurrency volume. Our routing logic identifies digital asset payloads at the terminal level and bifurcates them directly to Nuvei/Simplex. Standard acquirers never touch or see a crypto transaction on their ledger.

AML / KYC Firewalled

Durbin Amendment Surcharging

For our retail clients utilizing the POSitiveRails surcharge logic, our terminal firmware automatically detects the BIN (Bank Identification Number) of the presented card. If a debit card is detected, the surcharge fee is instantly suppressed to ensure absolute compliance with federal law and Visa/MC operating rules.
